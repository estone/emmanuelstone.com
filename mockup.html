The Maker

Built the old-fashioned way.

My name is Emmanuel, and I build acoustic guitars, ukuleles, and related instruments from my workshop near Miltown Malbay in County Clare.

I was trained in traditional lutherie techniques by Geza Burghardt in Vancouver, Canada, and have carried those methods back to Ireland. Every instrument leaves the bench having been made one at a time, with hand tools, patience, and french polish.

I also offer a range of repair and setup services, bringing your instrument back to its best with the same attention to craft.
